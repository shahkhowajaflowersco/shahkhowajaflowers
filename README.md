A modern flower e-commerce website with a WhatsApp-based ordering system, built for a local florist business in Karachi, Pakistan.

---

## 🌺 About

Petal & Co. is an online flower shop where customers can browse fresh flowers and place orders directly via WhatsApp. No payment gateway, no complicated checkout — just pick your flowers, fill in your details, and your order lands straight in our WhatsApp chat.

## ✨ Features

- Browse flowers by category — Roses, Bouquets, Seasonal, Plants
- Order via WhatsApp with auto-filled order messages
- Occasion-based ordering — Wedding, Birthday, Anniversary, Graduation & more
- Same day delivery available in Karachi (order before 2 PM)
- Free gift card message with every order
- Custom order & bulk order support
